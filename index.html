About Me

For as long as I can remember, I’ve been a tinkerer. Taking things apart and putting them back together again - the thrill of seeing something you’ve built yourself working the way you want it to - was a source of endless fascination for me as a kid, and it continues to drive my interests in computing and mathematics today.

My passions are rooted in that childhood tinkering, but my experience with theatre in high school has been a driving force in my views of the importance of arts in parallel with science and technology; I’ll forever be a STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, Mathematics) evangelist, and in an age where the moral and ethical implications of automation and machine learning are so prominent, a solid background in humanities is essentially in developing ethical software.
